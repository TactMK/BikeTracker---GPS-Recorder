BikeTracker - GPS Recorder privacy policy

General information

BikeTracker - GPS Recorder app is all apps released in the AppStore under Ilya Klimenko's personal account. Your privacy is important to us. BikeTracker - GPS Recorder policy is to respect your privacy.

Storage and synchronization

The app stores your data locally on the devices you use it on. The app does not use third-party storage, processing, or synchronization services.

Protection of certain personal information

We do not use, analyze, or use third-party programs to analyze your data.

Apple Health data

BikeTracker - GPS Recorder app does not read or analyse health or training data. It can only add cycling sessions, cycling distance, speed data and dates
